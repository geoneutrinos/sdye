Title: Low Energy Neutrino Astrophysics- LENA

Abstract: Geoneutrinos recently have become rather popular especially after KamLAND has detected first events. The question is now if one can resolve the angular distribution of these neutrinos in order to locate radioactive elements in the earth. In the talk some theoretical results as to how an angular distribution spectrum of geoneutrinos might look like will be presented. Conclusively the results shall be examined in the light of their experimental detectability with the large volume liquid scintillator detector LENA.
