Neutrino Sciences 2007

The Deep Ocean Antineutrino Observatory

Honolulu, Hawaii

March 23-25, 2007













The Center for Neutrino Sciences announces Neutrino Sciences 2007 on March 23-25, 2007 at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. This second in a series of international conferences on neutrino sciences highlights the deep ocean antineutrino observatory. Conference sessions dedicated to geo-neutrinos, reactor neutrinos, nuclear monitoring, and technical and collaboration forming issues are scheduled.
